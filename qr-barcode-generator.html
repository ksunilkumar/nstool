QR Code Customization

📖 How to use the QR Code Maker

Step 1: Enter your Content (a website URL, simple text, or contact information).

Step 2: Customize your QR Code by changing the Foreground and Background Colors to match your brand.

Step 3: Optional: Add a Title Text (like "Scan Me!") and upload a Center Logo to overlay directly onto the QR code.

Step 4: Click "Download High-Res QR" to save your custom code as a crisp, print-ready image file.

Barcode Generator

📖 How to use the Barcode Generator

Step 1: Select the required Format standard (e.g., CODE128 for alphanumeric, EAN-13, or UPC for products).

Step 2: Enter the numeric or alphanumeric Value to be encoded into the barcode lines.

Step 3: Fine-tune the appearance using the Width and Height sliders.

Step 4: Download the final barcode as either a rasterized PNG or a scalable vector SVG file for professional printing.
