Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

Is this PDF Converter truly free to use?

Yes, our document converter is entirely free with no hourly limits, no subscription fees, and no frustrating watermarks stamped on your final files.

How is 'Extract Image' different from 'PDF to Image'?

PDF to Image takes a 'screenshot' of the entire PDF page (including text and formatting) and turns it into a JPG. Extract Image searches the PDF for actual picture files (like inserted photos) and pulls them out individually in their original uncompressed format.

Do you save my converted PDFs?

No! All processing is executed by your own web browser. Your sensitive files never leave your computer or get uploaded to our cloud servers.
