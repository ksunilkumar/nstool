Transform your written text into highly realistic, natural-sounding audio instantly. Features of Our Voice Generator

  • Indian Accents Prioritized: The tool automatically highlights the best `en-IN` Indian English voices available on your device, making it perfect for regional content creators.
  • Download MP3: Quickly convert short texts and click "Download MP3" to save the audio file directly to your device for video voiceovers or podcasts.

How to Convert Text to Audio Online

Simply paste your script into the text area. Choose your preferred voice from the dropdown menu—look for labels like (Male) or (Female). Adjust the speed and pitch sliders if necessary, and click Speak Text to preview. Once satisfied, use the Download MP3 button for short clips.

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

Is this Text to Audio converter completely free?

Yes! Our TTS tool relies on the built-in SpeechSynthesis APIs and free online bridges to generate voices at absolutely zero cost to you.

Can I download the Text to Speech as an MP3 file?

Yes, you can download short text snippets (under 200 characters) as an MP3 file by clicking the 'Download MP3' button. For much longer texts, we recommend using a free system audio recorder while the tool plays the voice.

Why don't I see Indian voices on my iPhone/Android?

The voices available depend entirely on your Operating System (Windows, macOS, Android, iOS) and Browser. If an Indian voice is installed on your device, our tool will automatically find it and bring it to the top of the list.
