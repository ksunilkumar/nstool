About Us

Welcome to Toolbox, your ultimate destination for high-quality, fast, and secure web-based utilities. We specialize in providing tools that make your digital life easier and more efficient, entirely inside your browser.

Our Mission

At Toolbox, our mission is to empower developers, designers, professionals, and daily internet users by offering a rich set of online utilities. We believe that tools like file conversion, PDF merging, barcode generation, and code formatting should be free, accessible, and fast, without compromising your privacy.

Why Choose Toolbox?

  • Privacy First: The majority of our tools process files and data directly inside your web browser. This means your files never leave your device, ensuring maximum privacy and security.
  • No Installation Required: Run tools instantly on any device (desktop, tablet, or mobile) without installing complex software.
  • Beautiful & Modern Design: A clean, responsive interface optimized for usability, speed, and comfort (including dark mode).
  • Completely Free: Access all premium tool capabilities without subscriptions or hidden costs. We sustain our services via clean, non-intrusive advertisements.

What We Offer

We provide a wide array of specialized tools designed for daily digital tasks:

  • File & Image Tools: Compress images, convert formats (like SVG, PNG, and JPG), and generate barcode/QR codes.
  • PDF Utilities: Merge multiple PDFs, convert PDF to images, and optimize document size.
  • Developer Utilities: Format and parse JSON, generate invoices, and text-to-audio converter.
